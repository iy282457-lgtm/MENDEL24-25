Performance Tasks

Mendel Jams

We started off the school year with a full swing as we showcase our talent for singing during our Jamming session entitled "The Mendel Jams". With the themes of 'Jologs' and 'Kalye' vibes. Class Mendel didn't miss a beat and made the party rolling.

Movie Trailer-Poster

In the field of filmmaking, class mendel once again took on the challenge to create their very own movie trailer and movie poster. With each group crafting their own unique twist, from thrilling comedy to heartfelt dramas, showcasing their ability to bring their creative imaginations to life.

School Hymn Rendition

"We are history in the making... " Every note of our school hymn echoes the legacy of those who came before us, the vibrance of who we are today, and the hope we hold for a brighter tomorrow. Rooted in the timeless composition of Batch 1988, Section Mendel reimagines this iconic anthem with a dynamic rock and pop twist.

Volcano Model Project

Another project completed by Class Mendel as they worked on their own version of a Volcano Model. Highlighting not only their understanding on Earth Science but also their creativity and craftsmanship.

Filming

Class Mendel takes creativity to new heights by coming together to write, produce, and film their very own short film. From brainstorming ideas to bringing their vision to life on screen, every member contributed their unique talents, turning a simple class project into a true cinematic collaboration.

Job Interview

The skills of Mendel learners are once again tested in a simulated job interview, alongside the other sections: Euclid, Newton, and Einstein. By creating resumes, cover letters, and participating in mock interviews, Mendel students gained confidence and practical tools for future careers.

HOPE Day

A day filled with laughter and excitement, Hope Day at the SHS department was a total success! Where Grade 11 students took the lead, creating a day full of fun games, with class Mendel's Bean With Me game stealing the spotlight, making it a day to remember.
